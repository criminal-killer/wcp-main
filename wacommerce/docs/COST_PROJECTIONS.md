## 3. Profit Margin
- **Gross Margin:** ~90% (Low infrastructure cost)
- **Net Margin:** ~70% (After marketing & support costs)

## 4. Rationale
- **Price Point:** $29 is the "magic number" for African and SE Asian SMEs. It's cheaper than Shopify ($39) but specifically solves the WhatsApp problem.
- **WhatsApp API:** We charge 0% markup. This is our differentiator against competitors like Wati/Interakt who charge $49+ PLUS markup.
- **Acquisition:** Low CAC due to organic social media loops (referrals, "Comment READY").
