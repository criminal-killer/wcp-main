## Free Trial
- 14 days of full Starter features
- No credit card required
- Auto-downgrades to Free plan after trial

## Free Plan (Post-Trial)
- 10 products, 20 orders/month, 50 contacts
- "Powered by Sella" watermark (cannot remove)
- Enough to test but not enough to run a real business
- Designed to convert to Starter

## Launch Discount
- Early bird: 30% off FOREVER with code `EARLY30`
- Starter: $29 → $20.30/mo forever
- Only for waitlist members
- Creates urgency + rewards early supporters

## Pricing on Landing Page
- Show ONLY Starter price ($29)
- Growth and Premium: "Coming Soon — Join waitlist for pricing"
- Waitlist form asks: "How much would you pay?" (market research)
- Adjust Growth/Premium pricing based on responses before launch

## Why $29 (Not $15 or $49)
- $15 feels too cheap for B2B tool (not taken seriously)
- $49 competes directly with AiSensy ($49) — bad position
- $29 is significantly cheaper than all competitors
- $29 × 100 customers = $2,900/mo (sustainable)
- $29 is affordable for African small businesses
- $29 × 12 = $348/year (less than 1 month of Wati)
