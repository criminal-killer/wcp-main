# Growth Strategy
# SELLA

---

## 1. The Core Growth Loop
**Customers buy on WhatsApp → Customer sees "Powered by Sella" → Customer becomes a Seller.**

## 2. Acquisition Channels

### Organic Social Media (Primary)
- **Strategy:** "Show, don't tell." Post screen-recordings of the bot in action.
- **Call to Action:** "Comment READY to get access."
- **Automation:** Comment bot monitors keywords and DMs users the waitlist link.

### Cold Outreach (Active)
- **Strategy:** Scraping business data (Instagram, Google Maps) in target markets.
- **Execution:** Automated emails/DMs offering a "14-day free WhatsApp store setup."

### Referral System (Viral)
- **Strategy:** Give months free for successful referrals.
- **Tiers:** 
  - 1 Referral: 1 Month Free
  - 5 Referrals: 3 Months Free
  - 10 Referrals: 1 Year Free (Growth Plan)

### Content Marketing
- **Strategy:** Build "Sella Academy" — free guides on how to sell on WhatsApp.
- **Focus:** SEO-optimized blogs (e.g., "How to use M-Pesa on WhatsApp").

## 3. Localization & Market Focus
- **Priority 1:** Kenya & Tanzania (M-Pesa dominance).
- **Priority 2:** Nigeria & Ghana (Paystack dominance).
- **Priority 3:** Southeast Asia (Indonesia, Philippines).

## 4. Retention (Stickiness)
- Once a business adds 10+ products and has active orders, churn is extremely low (< 5%).
- **Onboarding:** "White-glove" setup for the first 100 users to ensure success.

---

## 5. Metrics to Watch
1. **Waitlist Signups:** Goal: 1,000 before launch.
2. **Conversion (Waitlist -> Paid):** Target: 5-10%.
3. **Activation Rate:** % of users who add their first product.
4. **Virality Coefficient:** Goal: > 0.3 (every 3 users bring 1 more).
